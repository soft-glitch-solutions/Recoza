Recoza is an open-source, South African green-tech mobile application that helps unemployed youth earn income by organising community recycling through trusted networks of friends, family, and neighbours.

The app turns informal recycling into dignified, structured work by helping collectors track items, plan weekly collections, and estimate earnings.

Anyone can join as a household user

Collectors invite friends and family as their recycling clients

Estimates weekly earnings

Allows households to switch collectors or become collectors themselves

All interactions stay inside the app to ensure safety, trust, and accountability.
